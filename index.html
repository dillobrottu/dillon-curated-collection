Welcome

This collection's purpose is to inform the user on how to effectively learn and practice music.
Many people often struggle with trying to get into music by not understanding where to start learning or how to improve.
It can be difficult as a beginner may not know where to begin looking to find the information they need.
This collection contains guides, references, and repositories for information on improving your musical skills.
Each category contains various sources that can either assist you in learning music or improve your practicing skills.
This collection can be used by following one of the three main categories:
Guides - References - Repositories

Category 1: Guides

Guides contain materials regarding instruction, lessons,
and exercises that assist the user in learning or practicing music. Each guide can give
you a targeted lesson or give you an exercise for you to refresh your learned
knowledge.

Category 2: References

References contain reliable material you can use as support and advice.
Each reference allows you to view learning material from a trusted source.
Each reference allows you to learn from an external source and gain your own insight into the material.

Category 3: Repositories

Repositories are information databases that contain a vast variety of material for you to browse.
Repositories contain a collection of information that you can use
to find material that may assist you in your learning.
